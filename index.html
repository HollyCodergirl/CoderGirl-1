LaunchCoderGirl Page

Welcome to the CoderGirl GitHub Page!

CoderGirl is a LaunchCode Community program. We are an all-women's programming meet-up that provides a space for women to explore topics in technology and programming while being supported by a community of like-minded women in tech. We are amplifying the voice of women in this field and are dedicated to providing them with career opportunities within it.

This is where you can find all there is to know about LaunchCoderGirl and LaunchCode Community events and happenings.
